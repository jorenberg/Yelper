      SUMMARY
====================
Yelp dataset contains 8 categories: [restaurants, shopping, nightlife, pets, hotelstravel, homeservices, beautysvc and active], which is crawled form Yelp.com website. Most of the users of these categories are from New York City.

Source of Data:
===============
1. Yelp, http://www.yelp.com/
